Sampada

Mentor

“Sampada, previously worked for 'Azim Premji Philanthropy Foundation' has been a part of BTCH since it’s inception. Nagendra Babu

Co-Founder

“Nagendra Babu, a three world record holder, also the Bangalore City Head of Zomato Feeding India. He has been associated with various NGOs for over four years in various capacities. Creating a Hunger Free Nation is his Dream and this led to the creation of 'Bethechange Hospete' a social innovation to solve various society problems with the help of youth. You can find him at functions and party halls. No, he won’t be partying there but would be collecting excess food to feed empty stomachs.”
