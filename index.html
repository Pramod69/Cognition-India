Our Philantrophist

“Sampada, previously worked for 'Azim Premji Philanthropy Foundation' has been a part of BTCH since it’s inception. Thus, laying the foundation along with other members, she has played a vital role in turning BTCH from an idea into reality. Having more than 13yrs of experience in the education sector as a Social Science teacher and working for a year and half as resource person for a reputed organisation, she has imbibed a greater understanding of the importance of education and health.”

Sampada, Mentor.

“Nagendra Babu, a three world record holder, also the Bangalore City Head of Zomato Feeding India. He has been associated with various NGOs for over four years in various capacities. Creating a Hunger Free Nation is his Dream and this led to the creation of 'Bethechange Hospete' a social innovation to solve various society problems with the help of youth. You can find him at functions and party halls. No, he won’t be partying there but would be collecting excess food to feed empty stomachs.”

Nagendra Babu, Co-Founder.
