Rural Development and Upskill






A society is developed only when its rural population is developed. So to promote upgrading of rural society we choose to work in the most interior rural parts.

We promote quality education by setting library, we visit rural schools to teach children english. We also admit under privilege girls to under go training classes for skill development and empower themselves. This in return will be helpful for them to financially support their family.
We also are looking forward to educate the farmers about modern agriculture methods and techniques along with proper implementation of organic cultivation.
We are keen on encouraging rural farmers, small scale vendors and suppliers to go digital transformation.
